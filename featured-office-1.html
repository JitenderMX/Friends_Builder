OVERVIEW

Friends Palak, a prime office space project by Friends Builders, offers a prestigious business environment in the heart of Agra. Located at Langre Ki Chowki, N.H.2, this project sets the stage for success.With construction completed between 2000 and 2002, Friends Palak boasts 54 meticulously designed units, providing a range of office spaces tailored to meet diverse business needs. Spanning over 23.7K square feet, the project offers ample space for productivity and growth.

Friends Palak has garnered immense popularity, with all units sold. Its strategic location along NH 2 ensures excellent connectivity and easy access to key commercial hubs. Experience the epitome of professional excellence at Friends Palak, where impeccable design and a thriving business environment converge to create an exceptional office space experience.
