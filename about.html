WE ARE
THE BUILDERS

Friends Builders Group, a highly respected real estate developer based in Agra, boasts an enduring legacy spanning over four decades. With a portfolio of 40+ residential and commercial projects, the company is known for impeccable quality and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction.

Founded by Mr. Sushil Kumar Agarwal, a revered industry stalwart, Friends Builders Group is synonymous with innovative designs, execution excellence, and a customer-centric approach. Under the dynamic leadership of Mr. Agarwal's sons, Ayush and Anurag Agarwal, the company evolves with modern technologies while upholding core values of trust, quality, and integrity.
