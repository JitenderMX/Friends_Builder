OVERVIEW

Sky Tower, a prestigious residential project by Friends Builders, redefines luxury living in the heart of Aligarh. Completed with utmost precision and attention to detail, this magnificent tower stands tall as a symbol of excellence.Featuring 2 to 4 bedroom apartments, Sky Tower offers a range of spacious and elegantly designed homes. With a total of 267 units, residents can indulge in a vibrant community and enjoy a high standard of living.

Spanning over 18,000 square feet, Sky Tower boasts impressive living spaces and state-of-the-art amenities. Its prime location in Aligarh provides easy access to educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and major transportation hubs.Experience the pinnacle of residential living at Sky Tower, where modern architecture, luxurious interiors, and a thriving community converge to create a truly exceptional living experience.
