WE ARE
THE BUILDERS

Friends Builders Group, a highly respected real estate developer based in Agra, boasts an enduring legacy spanning over four decades. With a portfolio of 40+ residential and commercial projects, the company is known for impeccable quality and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction.

Founded by Mr. Sushil Kumar Agarwal, a revered industry stalwart, Friends Builders Group is synonymous with innovative designs, execution excellence, and a customer-centric approach. Under the dynamic leadership of Mr. Agarwal's sons, Ayush and Anurag Agarwal, the company evolves with modern technologies while upholding core values of trust, quality, and integrity.

WE BELIEVE IN

Foster Sustainable Development

We are committed to creating spaces that not only cater to the present but also preserve the future. Embracing eco-friendly practices, using renewable materials, and incorporating energy-efficient solutions, we envision a greener tomorrow. At Friends Builders, sustainability is at the core of our mission to build a better world.

Promotes Inclusivity

We firmly believe in fostering an environment where diversity is celebrated and embraced. We strive to create spaces that cater to all individuals, regardless of their backgrounds or abilities. Through accessible design and thoughtful planning, we ensure that everyone feels welcome and valued, cultivating a sense of belonging within our communities.

Enhance Livelihood

We believe in empowering local communities and contributing to sustainable livelihoods. Through our projects, we create employment opportunities, support local businesses, and foster economic growth. We aim to uplift lives by building spaces that not only elevate living standards but also enrich the surrounding neighbourhoods, fostering a thriving and inclusive environment.

Affordable Living

We stand firmly for affordable living, recognizing that everyone deserves a comfortable and secure home without compromising on quality. We are committed to crafting residential spaces that cater to various budgetary needs, ensuring that our clients experience the joy of homeownership while enjoying the best value for their investment.
